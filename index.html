About Us

In this connected era, we enjoy the convenience of technology, but often overlook our parents struggling behind us. Unable to book doctor appointments, missing video calls with grandchildren, or losing savings to scams...

Silver Link was born from guilt and responsibility. We hope to be the "Silver Link" connecting children and parents. We are dedicated to bridging this digital divide through professional, patient service, allowing every senior to enjoy digital life with dignity, and every child to work away from home with peace of mind.

Our Values: Respect, Warmth, New Filial Piety.

Strict Screening

Privacy Promise

Strict NDAs signed. No viewing of unrelated private info. Funds operations must be authorized by children or seniors.

Empathy Training

FAQ

üõ°Ô∏è Security & Privacy

Is in-home service safe? How do you screen staff?

Every Silver Link assistant undergoes strict background checks: ID verification, no criminal record, and health certificate. Services are recorded (with consent) to ensure transparency.

Will my parents' privacy be leaked?

Absolutely not. We sign strict NDAs and only view the minimum information needed to solve the problem. For example, when teaching how to send photos, we do not browse other albums.

üí∞ Pricing & Refunds

Is there a fee if the senior refuses service on-site?

If service cannot proceed due to strong resistance, we do not charge the service fee, only a small travel stipend (approx. ¬•30-50).

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Free cancellation/rescheduling 4+ hours before service. Within 4 hours, a small fee may apply.
